ABOUT ASHAYEE

Ashayee's main motive is to send alerts to people who can act in time. Instant alerts can provide immediate help and save women from becoming a victim of attacks. Various apps help to detect the safety levels in your area.The creators of Ashayee are-Rajanya Giri,Manisha Banerjee,Ishan Majumder,Ritobrata Dey.

Ashayee is one of the fast working women safety apps.Personal Safety Apps are designed specifically for you to communicate with others that you may need help. These apps can provide peace of mind, immediate communication, and access to help in an emergency.Women Safety app is the best app to inform and update your close ones if you are in an unsafe place. The app is the fastest and easiest way to update your close ones about your location and other details.
